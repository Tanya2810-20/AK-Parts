Domestic Shipping Policy

Shipment Processing Times

All Orders are processed within 3-5 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or public holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your Order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your Orders will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Shipping Method: Delhivery Surface or another trusted carrier.

Shipment Cost: Varies depending on the total weight and shipping address.

Estimated Delivery Time: 3-7 business days within India, subject to location and carrier performance.

Delivery times are estimates only. Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather, strikes, or logistical challenges. We recommend allowing extra time during peak seasons.

Shipment to P.O. Boxes or APO/FPO Addresses

Currently, we ship only to addresses within India and Indian territories.

Once your Order is shipped, You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email containing a tracking number. Tracking information will become active within 24 hours, allowing You to monitor your shipment’s progress.
